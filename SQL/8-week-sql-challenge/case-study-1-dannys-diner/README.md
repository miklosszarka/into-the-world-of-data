# Case Study 1 – Danny's Diner

## Project Overview

This case study is part of the **8 Week SQL Challenge** created by **Danny Ma**.

The goal of this project is to analyze customer purchasing behavior, spending patterns, and menu preferences using SQL.

---

## Business Problem

Danny wants to use his restaurant sales and membership data to better understand customer behavior and improve his business decisions.

This analysis focuses on answering practical business questions such as:
- Who are the most valuable customers?
- What are the most popular menu items?
- How often do customers visit?
- How does membership affect purchasing behavior?
