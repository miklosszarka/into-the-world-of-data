# Digital Takeover

## Project Overview
**Digital Takeover** is an ongoing Tableau project built around the **Share of US Ad Spend** dataset.

The dataset covers advertising spending trends in the United States from **2012 to 2022**, including a **projected outlook for 2023 and 2024**.

The purpose of this project is to experiment with multiple ways of visualizing the same dataset in order to improve data storytelling, strengthen visualization skills, and identify which chart types communicate the insights most effectively.

## Objectives
- Explore the evolution of advertising spending in the U.S.
- Compare traditional and digital advertising trends
- Practice creating multiple visualizations from the same dataset
- Evaluate which visual formats are the clearest and most insightful

## Focus Areas
- Share of advertising spend over time
- Trend comparison across media channels
- Historical patterns vs. projected future changes
- Data storytelling through visualization design
